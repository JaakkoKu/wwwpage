Vocations

Paladin

Paladins are holy warriors, skilled in both melee combat and divine magic, serving as protectors of the weak.

Mage

Sorcerers harness arcane powers, specializing in spells of destruction, control, and utility, mastering the elements.

Knight

Knights are valiant melee fighters, excelling in defense and close combat, known for their unyielding strength.

Druid

Druids are nature's guardians, wielding powers to heal, protect, and control the forces of the natural world.
